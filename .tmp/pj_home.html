Experience unbeatable durability and efficiency with PJ Trailers' heavy-duty dump trailers. Built for tough jobs, they handle gravel, dirt, scrap, and debris with ease. Shop All Car Haulers

Transporting your car is effortless with our car hauler trailers, built for safety, durability, and convenience. Featuring a wide deck, drive-over fenders, and removable fender options, these trailers offer easy loading and unloading. Designed for high load capacity, they can transport cars, trucks, and heavy equipment with stability and control on smooth or rough terrain. Built tough for demanding conditions, our trailers ensure smooth handling and enhanced safety with secure tie-downs and a low deck height. PJ Trailers’ car haulers make for easy loading with ramps or tilting decks, ensuring a hassle-free experience. Whether for short trips or long hauls, our car haulers provide the reliability and versatility you need.

Shop All Tilt Trailers

No ramps needed— it starts with PJ Trailers' premium tilt trailers. Hydraulic cylinders provide smooth, controlled tilting, while the optimized deck design reduces load angles for safer operation. Available in multiple configurations, including single-axle and gooseneck models, these trailers handle loads from 5,200 to 28,000 lbs. Built for versatility, they transport everything from heavy equipment and machinery to vehicles and construction materials. Durable materials ensure stability on rough terrain, cutting costs by eliminating extra loading equipment. A tilt trailer is a smart investment that boosts efficiency, reduces hassle, and delivers lasting value.
